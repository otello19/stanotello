Kovacic is Chelsea Player of the Year

Mason Mount, Tammy Abraham and Giroud were better...

Although Kovacic impressed me last season, I was pretty surprised he was voted player of the year. My reason was mostly because he was not as consistent and effective as some other players. I had a shortlist of five players for the award. Unfortunately, the fifth player it. They are in this particular order:

  1. Mason Mount,
  2. Ceasar Azpilaceuta,
  3. Tammy Abraham,
  4. Olivier Giroud, and
  5. Mateo Kovacic.

It's all said then, no undermining of his effort and contribution last season, which was Ballack-sque at times. Let's go Blues!
